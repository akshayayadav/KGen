Part-whole relations are important in many domains.
Part-whole relations typically receive less attention than subsumption relation.
In this paper we describe a method for finding part-whole relations.
a method consists of two steps: applying phrase patterns for both explicit and implicit part-whole relations to find part-whole relation instances.

We show results of applying a method to a domain of finding sources of carcinogens.
